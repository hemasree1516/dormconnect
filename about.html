About DormConnect

Purpose of the Website

DormConnect is designed to help college girls at GRIET find hostels easily and reliably. The platform provides verified reviews, room availability, food quality ratings, and feedback from seniors — solving real problems faced during hostel hunting.

Inspiration & Problem Solving

Inspired by personal experiences, we created DormConnect to make hostel selection easier. By integrating real-time search, verified user reviews, and filters based on needs, we’ve overcome the lack of reliable hostel info.
